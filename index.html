Intro

Why Nu Alpha Phi

Brotherhood.

“One thing you can’t get or replicate in any club or group is the close bond as brothers we share. I love the fact that no matter the time or place, there will always be a Brother selfless enough to help.” – Jing “Nexus” Bao

Growth.

“I saw an opportunity to develop not only myself but something that was bigger than any one individual. I wanted to be able to look back at my college career and say I took full advantage of what Greek Life had to offer and have a brotherhood, a family, that would last me way beyond just college.” – Max “Zuko” Kohutich

Family.

“Being a brother of Nu Alpha Phi means that you can talk to any brother, present or past, without hesitation or awkwardness. There is a world of connections in different veins of the world present in Nu Alpha Phi, and they are all within your grasp." – Karun “Avatar” Sekhar

History

  • 1994

    Our Humble Beginnings

    On March 18th, 1994 eight great men founded the first chapter of Nu Alpha Phi Fraternity Inc. at SUNY Albany.

  • 2008

    A Legend is Born

    On September 21st, 2008, eight bold men founded the eighth chapter of Nu Alpha Phi Fraternity Inc. at Stevens Institute of Technology.

  • Present

    Where we are Now ...

    The fraternity professes to create a bond of brotherhood passed down from generation to generation, including lasting relationships between alumni and currently active members.

Chapter Wars 2018!

Annual Brotherhood Sports Event!

Every year, Nu Alpha Phi organizes a sports olympic, brother against brother in sports like softball, basketball, and volleyball between all nine chapters. Brothers from every generation, young and old, practice to prepare themselves for this annual event.

N.A.Pex for Youth!

For the Kids!

Enjoy a night of entertainment as students across campus unite to raise money for "Apex for Youth," an organization which delivers possibilities to underserved Asian youth in NYC. It recruits volunteers to be positive role models who guide and inspire youth to become confident young adults who are ready for college and give back to the community. Nu Alpha Phi is working hard to provide a Benefit Concert in which we hope to bring together fellow students and friends to raise money for this great cause.
